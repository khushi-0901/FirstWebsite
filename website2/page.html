What are we?

How many of us wonder, "Oh! I wish this story had an averse ending", Or, "Oh! how I wish this lasted a bit longer, ended so abrupt". And to that, we have our own little tell tales, In mediocre conversations we as humans present such brilliant ideas and perspectives that its just unfair to not document it or bring them to the light of day.This right here is a hub consisting of blogs,articles, mini tales, fan fictions, literary works that are written by the author, along with which there's an emerging collaborative blog tightening its bearings to build a strong,assorted forum of writers, thinkers and artists.
